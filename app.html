FIZZBUZZ

Two numbers between 1 and 100 are entered, the first number will be assigned to "fizz" and the second number assigned to "buzz". Numbers between 1 to 100 will be displayed. Numbers divisible by "fizz" will display as "FIZZ". Numbers divisible by "buzz" will display as "BUZZ". Numbers divisible by both numbers will display as "FIZZBUZZ".
